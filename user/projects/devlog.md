Welcome to this site. 

In my thinking and in my work I find it challenging to organize/delineate things by topic. I like seeing connections, and chasing tangents, a way of thinking that has undeniable and immeasurable value. But when you’re not disciplined, it usually leads to not getting a lot done.

Creating this site is an exercise in constraint, in limiting the scope of an endeavor such that its result is coherent and focused.
I’m building a new room. It’s separate from my bedroom, or the kitchen, or the living room, or the abstract spaces where I do one specific activity.

Like most people (?), I wish that software and digital space were a very natural extension of my own mind, my own self really.
But a whole self is very complicated to interface with, and not something easily reflected in the primitive constructs of which I’m capable of producing—or the constructs that define the current personal computing paradigm. 

A digital space that’s more cognitively ergonomic… that’s a separate room. This room is simple, and it’s only about developing software.

It’s static site generator.
